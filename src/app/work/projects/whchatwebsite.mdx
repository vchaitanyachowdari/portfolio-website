## Overview

**Wh Chat** is a modern multi-page business platform designed to present enterprise messaging solutions powered by the WhatsApp Business API.

The project focuses on combining conversion-driven marketing strategy with modern frontend architecture, enabling businesses to understand, evaluate, and adopt WhatsApp automation solutions through interactive storytelling, structured content, and real-time engagement tools.

Built using Next.js 15 with App Router, the platform demonstrates scalable architecture optimized for performance, accessibility, and SEO visibility.

---

## Core Objectives

- Create a conversion-focused digital presence for WhatsApp Business solutions
- Educate users through structured industry-specific messaging
- Enable lead capture and engagement through interactive tools
- Build scalable architecture aligned with enterprise standards
- Deliver high-performance experience across all devices
