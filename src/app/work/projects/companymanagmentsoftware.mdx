## Overview

**VC AI Creator CMS** is a unified agency operating system designed to centralize workflows across clients, teams, vendors, finance, HR, marketing, and product delivery.

Rather than relying on disconnected tools, the platform integrates multiple operational layers into one ecosystem — enabling agencies and development studios to manage their entire business lifecycle from a single interface.

The system is built using modern frontend architecture and serverless backend infrastructure, with AI-powered automation embedded across core workflows.

---

## Core Objectives

- Replace fragmented agency tooling with a unified platform
- Provide role-based multi-portal access for different stakeholders
- Enable scalable project and operational management
- Integrate AI assistance across workflows
- Maintain enterprise-grade security with granular permissions

---

## Multi-Portal Architecture

The application is divided into three independent portals, each tailored to specific user groups.

| Portal | Login Route | Dashboard | Target Users |
|--------|-------------|-----------|--------------|
| Team Portal | /team/login | /dashboard | Admins, Project Managers, Developers |
| Client Portal | /client/login | /client/dashboard | Clients and stakeholders |
| Vendor Portal | /vendor/login | /vendor/dashboard | External vendors and collaborators |

Each portal has dedicated layouts, permission systems, and dashboards while sharing a unified backend.

## Role Hierarchy & Permissions

### Role Structure

- Super Admin — Full system access and configuration
- Admin — HR, marketing, finance, and system management
- Project Manager — Projects, clients, invoices, vendors
- Developer — Assigned tasks, time tracking, collaboration

### Department-Based Access

Granular page-level permissions configurable by department:

- Administration
- Marketing
- Accounting
- Sales
- Development Engineers
- R&D
- HR

Row-Level Security ensures controlled access to sensitive data.
