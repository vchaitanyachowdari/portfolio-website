## The Client: A 4-Person Consulting Firm

*Details anonymized to protect client confidentiality.*

The client is a 4-person management consulting firm serving mid-market Indian businesses. Their core services: strategy consulting, financial modeling, and operational review.

**Their situation before we started:**
- 60% of team time on non-billable work: proposal writing, research, reporting, admin
- Client retention was good (85%) but growth was flat because the team couldn't scale bandwidth
- Revenue: ₹18 lakhs/month from 6 retainer clients
- Leadership was spending 15 hours/week on tasks that weren't strategy

They came to us with a simple brief: **"We need to grow without hiring."**

---

## Week 1: Discovery and Mapping

We spent the first week not building anything. We documented every process in the business:
- How new client leads are handled
- How proposals are written
- How research is conducted for client work
- How reports are delivered
- How invoices are followed up
- How internal communication happens

We identified **23 distinct processes** that were being handled manually. Of those, we scored 11 as viable for automation (high frequency + low exception rate). We prioritized the top 4 for Week 1-6 implementation.

---

## Week 2-3: Proposal Automation

**Before:** Each proposal took 4-6 hours of senior consultant time. Research, writing, formatting.

**Built:**
- Discovery call intake form in Notion
- Make.com workflow: form submission → OpenAI generates proposal draft using client's templates and tone
- Draft delivered to Google Docs in their brand template
- Human review and refinement: 45 minutes

**Result after 2 weeks:**
- 4 proposals generated
- Average senior time per proposal: 50 minutes (was 5 hours)
- Client feedback on quality: "Identical to what we'd write ourselves"

---

## Week 3-4: Research Intelligence System

**Before:** Junior consultants spent 60-70% of their time on research. Google, industry reports, competitor websites — manually synthesized into Notion pages.

**Built:**
- n8n research agent: inputs a company name or industry → queries web, extracts relevant content, AI synthesizes into structured research brief
- Integrated with their existing Notion workspace

**Result:**
- Research brief turnaround: 4 hours → 25 minutes
- Junior consultant time on research: 70% → 20% of their week
- Freed capacity redirected to client-facing deliverable improvement

---

## Week 4-5: Automated Client Reporting

**Before:** 1 full day per week generating client status reports from data pulled manually from their financial models and project trackers.

**Built:**
- Google Sheets API → n8n → OpenAI generates narrative commentary → formatted Notion report → email delivered

**Result:**
- Reporting time: 8 hours/week → 1 hour/week (review and approval only)
- Reports delivered every Friday at 7 AM automatically, before the team even starts work

---

## Week 5-6: Invoice and Payment Follow-Up

**Before:** Managing invoice status and chasing late payments was a constant source of awkwardness. A team admin tracked it manually in a spreadsheet.

**Built:**
- New invoice created in accounting software → triggers Make.com webhook → 7-day follow-up sequence for unpaid invoices → friendly AI-generated reminder emails

**Result:**
- Average days to payment: 32 days → 21 days
- One late payment recovered automatically (₹85,000) that had been "forgotten" in the manual system

---

## The 6-Week Summary

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| Non-billable time | 60% | 28% | -32pp |
| Proposal time | 5 hrs | 50 min | -83% |
| Research time | 4 hrs | 25 min | -90% |
| Reporting time | 8 hrs/wk | 1 hr/wk | -88% |
| Avg days to payment | 32 days | 21 days | -34% |

The freed capacity from these 4 automations unlocked **80+ additional billable hours per month** across the team. At their billing rate, that's **₹4-5 lakhs/month in incremental revenue potential** without hiring.
